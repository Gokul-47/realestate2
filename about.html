Our Journey

Company History & Timeline

2009 - Humble Beginnings

Stackly was founded by a group of passionate real estate professionals with a vision to revolutionize the industry. Starting with a small team of 5, we focused on personalized service and building trust.

2012 - Expansion Phase

We expanded our operations to 5 major cities and grew our team to 35 professionals. Our commitment to excellence earned us our first industry recognition and certification.

2015 - Digital Transformation

Introduced cutting-edge digital platform for property listings and client management. This innovation enhanced our service delivery and solidified our position as an industry leader.

2018 - Reaching Milestones

Celebrated 3000+ successful transactions and expanded to 12 major markets. Our team grew to 100+ professionals with expertise across all property types and markets.

2021 - Global Recognition

Received prestigious industry awards for innovation, customer service, and market leadership. Started international expansion and established partnerships across multiple countries.

2024 - Present Excellence

Now managing 5000+ properties with 3500+ satisfied clients and 150+ expert agents. Continuing to innovate with AI-powered solutions and sustainable development initiatives.
